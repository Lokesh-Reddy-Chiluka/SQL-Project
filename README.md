# SQL Project: Chinook Music Store Analysis

### 🎯 Business Objective
The goal of this project was to analyze the Chinook Music Store database to uncover insights into customer purchasing patterns, artist popularity, and regional sales performance. This analysis helps the business understand which genres drive the most revenue and which countries offer the highest growth potential.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
- **Language:** SQL (PostgreSQL/MySQL)
- **Complex Queries:** Utilized **Joins**, **CTEs (Common Table Expressions)**, and **Subqueries** to aggregate data across multiple tables.
- **Aggregations:** Used `GROUP BY`, `HAVING`, and `Window Functions` to rank artists and calculate running totals.
- **Reporting:** Exported query results to visualize key findings in the final presentation.

---

### 📈 Key Insights & Findings
- **Top Markets:** Identified the USA, Canada, and Brazil as the highest-revenue-generating regions.
- **Genre Popularity:** Confirmed that **Rock** is the dominant genre, accounting for the largest share of total track sales.
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** Segmented customers based on total spending to identify "VIP" clients for targeted marketing campaigns.
